About Me

As a Junior Manual Tester, I am dedicated to improving software quality through meticulous testing and analytical problem-solving. My journey in the tech world began with a solid foundation in computer science and programming from a technical high school, which I further enhanced by completing a comprehensive manual testing course at Coders Lab.

My GitHub repositories showcase my interest in various testing tools and methodologies, including Selenium WebDriver, TestNG, and JUnit. I've developed projects that demonstrate my skills in test automation, particularly for web applications.

I'm constantly expanding my skill set, with a focus on:

  • Manual and automated testing techniques
  • Test case design and execution
  • Bug reporting and documentation
  • Familiarity with Agile methodologies

My goal is to contribute to challenging projects where I can apply my analytical skills, learn from experienced professionals, and grow as a tester. I'm particularly interested in opportunities that allow me to bridge the gap between manual and automated testing, leveraging tools like Selenium to enhance test coverage and efficiency.

I bring to the table not just technical skills, but also excellent communication abilities, a keen eye for detail, and a proactive approach to problem-solving. I'm eager to join a dynamic team where I can contribute to creating high-quality software products while continuously learning and improving my craft.
